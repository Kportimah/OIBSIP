Hardware is also a skill that all IT STUDENTS must obtain

Foundation for Deeper Learning: Computer hardware provides the groundwork for understanding software, networking, and other IT concepts. Knowing the components and how they interact helps you grasp more complex topics. Troubleshooting and Problem-Solving: When software or network issues arise, having a basic understanding of hardware can help you identify potential problems and troubleshoot more effectively. Career Opportunities: Many IT roles, from system administration to technical support, require knowledge of computer hardware. This can open up a wider range of career paths. Informed Decision Making: Whether you're building a personal computer or advising a client, understanding hardware can help you make informed decisions about components, compatibility, and performance. Enhanced Problem-Solving Skills: Troubleshooting hardware issues can develop your analytical and problem-solving abilities, which are valuable skills in any IT career.
